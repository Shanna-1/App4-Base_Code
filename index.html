Chickenpox or Measles?

Are you feeling like you might have the chickenpox but wonder if it is measles. Answer a few short questions to find out!

Do you have a runny nose?

Yes No

Results

If your reddish spots are on your stomach, chest, face, and back, spots are turning into blisters, fatigued and have a runny nose you probably have chickenpox. If your spots are clumped around your forehead in your mouth, have red inflamed eyes, and have hacking cough or sore throat you probably have the measles.
